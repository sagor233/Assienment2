Frequently Asked Questions

Event design and event planning work hand in hand to deliver a successful event. Events by Knight offer tailor made events. This means we incorporate both event design and event planning to create that perfect event.

We recommend starting the planning process at least 3-6 months in advance for most events. For larger events like weddings or corporate conferences, we suggest 6-12 months to ensure availability of your preferred date and adequate time for detailed planning.

Our cancellation policy varies by package. Generally, cancellations made 90 days or more before the event receive a full refund minus a processing fee. Cancellations within 30-89 days receive a 50% refund. Within 30 days, deposits are non-refundable, but we can discuss rescheduling options.

We provide detailed budget breakdowns at the start and maintain transparent communication throughout the planning process. Our experienced team helps prioritize expenses, suggests cost-effective alternatives, and provides regular budget updates to ensure you stay within your financial goals.
